Detailed Features

 Attendance We provide multiple options to mark student attendance and staff attendance (using panel, mobile application and RFID reader) to mark and track attendance of every member quickly in single click.

 Admission Manage student’s admissions effectively with advanced module to reduce time and paper work.

 Manage Users Manage every single student and staff member seamlessly. We provide advanced configuration and search options to schools to manage every single member accordingly.

 360 deg Profile View View every single student and staff member profile in 360 deg Profile view. Get complete information and view at one place.

 Manage Classes, Courses and Batches Manage every single class, courses and batches at one place with advanced features and functions.

 Advanced Search Filters We provide multiple filters to sort data that can help to sort and search data with in a second.

 Examination Manage all exam results, set subjects and marks according. Generate report card for students of your choice. Track student’s exam performance anytime.

 Multiple Export Options Export data using multiple options like: csv, excel, pdf, direct table copy.

 Generate Report Cards Generate exam report card of every single student for every exam. Combine exam types, assign weightage for each type and generate report card.

 Manage Accounting Manage income and expense separately. Get a P/L Statement auto generated by system. Export data in XML for tally inputs.

 Payment and Payroll Manage payment slip’s and payrolls of every single staff member. Maintain record for pay slips, generate pay slips and check record of pay slips anytime you want.

 Fee Management Manage student’s fee and maintain collection of total fees. Generate fee slips. Filter records as you want.

 Custom Reports and Certificates Generate custom reports of modules like payment, attendance, payroll and 12+ reports. Also Issue certificates like character, bonafide and other in clicks.

 Finance Management Manage amount spend, fees collection, all type of incomes and expenses at one place. Maintain record of every amount spend and earn.

 Backup and Restore Reduce the risk of losing data. Take backup of data and restore it anytime.

 Web & Mobile platforms We provide multi-platform software that works on web and Mobile application. You can use it on any device. In Other OS use browser to access software.

 Transport Management Manage your transport system effectively. Update bus route for every bus, maintain driver’s detail, vehicle documents, notifications for important dates and students in bus route.

 Time Table Manage Time table of every class and for every faculty. Manage substitute teacher during off work.

 Download Center Provide syllabus, assignment, useful content, study material to students.

 Hostel Management Manage hostel rooms, fees, students, warden, complains and other details. Customize every hostel facility and access everything about hostel at one place.

 Library Management Manage every book, issued book, due date of book and other details of library. Assign controls to librarian.

 Inventory Management Manage every inventory item with issue details and vendor list.

 Application Messaging System Do important messages on mobile applications. Create group or send individual messages.

 Push Notifications Send push notifications on mobile applications about every single activity.

 Email Integration Send automatic emails regarding important activities and custom emails too.

 School Website CMS Manage school website from panel. Update News, notices and content on website.

 News Feed Manage News feed of school. Show important News on login screen and on website too.

 Events Send events alert to every member in real time. Create event for teachers or for every member.

 Calendar Set calendar for holiday’s and activities.

 Homework Management Manage homework for each class and mark complete or incomplete to manage student progress report.

 Informative Dashboard Get important data with analyzed graphs and numbers form on dashboard to save time and effort.

 Analyzed Graphs Get the analyzed data graphs of many modules that will help to understand terms in a better way.

 To Do List Add future tasks that will come as a reminder on a dashboard.

 Visitors Management Manage visitors on daily basis. Get data about visitors at your panel to get clear idea of visitor purpose and count.

 Complaint Box Get complaints from members and parents from website and from their panel on your panel to make things better.

 Front Office Manage front office activities. Assign user permissions to the receptionist and track front office activities.

 HR Management Manage HR part of school. Hire new faculties, manage payroll, staff attendance, designations, departments and staff leave.

 Bus Tracking Members can track bus location and route. Drivers get ’SOS emergency button’ to call on emergency number.

 Themes Change the view of the panel to work with different theme options.

 Modules Control School can manage modules and show only modules to everyone those they want to show.
